VeriDate App

VeriDate features a scanning tool to instantly verify expiration dates and product authenticity, empowering consumers to make safe purchasing decisions. Additionally, the app includes an inventory management system to help users efficiently track stock levels, reducing waste and ensuring shelves are stocked with safe products.

It will have a flagging feature which will allow users to report suspicious products, prompting immediate action. Lastly, it is designed with a store locator to help users find verified and compliant spaza shops nearby.
