Our Journey

VeriDate, powered by TECHNNOVATION SOLUTIONS PTY (LTD), was founded by Sinawo Siqebengu in January 2024. The company is dedicated to developing innovative solutions that address critical community needs. The flagship offering, VeriDate, was inspired by the urgent need to address the issue of expired and counterfeit products plaguing our communities, which have led to severe health consequences. VeriDate has achieved significant milestones, including participation in the Hult Prize Nairobi Summit and securing funding from the East London Industrial Zone.

Vision

We envision a thriving informal sector transformed by digital innovation adding value to society.

Mission

Reimagine profitability by creating cutting-edge technology that builds inclusive digital economies that foster quality and safety.
